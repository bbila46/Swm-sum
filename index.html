📄 CASE FILE: Patient 0007

A 17-year-old arrives at SWM with persistent nausea, fatigue, and dizziness after several hours in the sun. No vomiting. Blood pressure slightly low.

Vitals: 38.5°C fever, dry skin, light-headed.

🧪 MEDICAL ANALYSIS

Early signs point to heat exhaustion with possible dehydration. Observation and IV fluids recommended. Symptoms not severe enough for heat stroke.
